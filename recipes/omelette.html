Omelette

Description

Like, you scramble the eggs but it's not scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cheese?
  • Other stuff
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Scramble like, 2 - 3 eggs in a bowl while you preheat your pan w/ oil.
  2. Add egg in pan. Optionally pull curds in from edge a little to add some texture. Ensure you can get the spatula under w/o tearing it. Add more oil if necessary.
  3. Put stuff in. Fold. Plate up.
